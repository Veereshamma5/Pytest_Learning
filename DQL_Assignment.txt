Question 1: Retrieve Information
Retrieve the names and ages of all students from the "Students" table.

Find the courses along with their credit hours from the "Courses" table.

Get the unique department names from the "Departments" table.
=============================

1. select concat(firstname, ' ', lastname) as Fullname, gender from students
2. select coursename, credits from Courses
3. select coursename, credits from Courses

Question 2: Filtering and Sorting
Retrieve the names of female students who are older than 20.

Find the courses with more than 3 credit hours, sorted by the course name.
============
select concat(firstname, ' ', lastname) as StudentName from students where gender='Female' and age>20
concat(firstname, ' ', lastname) as Fullname
